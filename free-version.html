What’s included

A clear overview of the Free Version.

Includes ads

The Free Version displays ads to help keep the platform accessible to everyone. Ads do not affect your learning sessions, but you may see them across the app interface.

Data Protection

We take privacy seriously. Your basic account data is protected and never shared without consent. You control what appears on your profile, and you can report/block users anytime.

Fixed Interface

The Free Version provides the core experience with a standard interface. Advanced personalization options may be available in premium tiers later.

Limited match proposals

You can send a limited number of match proposals in a given time period. This helps keep matching fair and reduces spam while we grow the community.
