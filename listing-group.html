About this group

A relaxed weekly conversation swap for English and Spanish learners. Each session is split into two halves: 30 minutes of English conversation followed by 30 minutes of Spanish. Members are friendly, supportive, and range from B1 to B2 level. No homework, no pressure — just real conversation practice in a welcoming space.
